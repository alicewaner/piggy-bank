How to Play

Welcome!

Hey there! In Piggy Bank you get to take care of cute animals, do fun quizzes, hang out with friends, and learn all about saving money. Here's everything you need to know!

Daily Tasks

Every day you'll have a list of tasks to do. Finish them and check them off to earn task credits! Complete enough tasks and you'll unlock water first, then food for your animals. Quiz correct answers count as credits too!

Quizzes

Want even more credits? Try taking a Math Quiz or an Encyclopedia Quiz from the Tasks screen! Every correct answer counts as a task credit toward unlocking food and water.

The Market

The Market is where you can buy and sell animals!

Buy a Baby Animal: Spend money from your bank to bring home an adorable new baby!

Sell an Adult Animal: Once your animal grows up, you can sell it to earn money back.

Your Barn

This is where all your animals live! Tap on any animal to see it up close. Hit the Feed button to give it 1 Food + 1 Water — that earns your animal 1 heart. You can feed each animal once a day. If your animal is happy, feeding gives a bonus heart too!

Mood

Your animals can be Happy or Sad. When they're happy, they earn an extra heart every time you feed them! Only a parent can change an animal's mood using the Parent PIN.

Hearts & Growing Up

Every time you feed your animal, it gets hearts. Once a baby collects 15 hearts, it grows into an adult — how cool is that? You'll see a shiny golden border around grown-up animals in your barn.

Your Bank

The Bank keeps your money safe! You can deposit money you earn or receive, and withdraw it when you want to buy something. The best part? Your savings earn interest over time, so the longer you save, the more your money grows!

Friends & Chat

Playing with friends is way more fun! Find other players on the Friends screen and add them. Once you're friends, you can peek at their barn, chat together, or even start a group chat. Type @Name in chat to send someone a private message!

Breeding Pen

Got two adult animals of the same type? Take them to the Breeding Pen and they'll have a brand-new baby together! After breeding, your animals need a little rest before they can do it again.
